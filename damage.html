Damage & Damage Types

Numerous damage types exist, each with unique properties. When a character rolls a success+ (20 or above on damage), roll once on the appropriate damage table. Every 5 above 20 grants an additional roll (25 = 2 rolls, 30 = 3 rolls). Types are grouped as Physical, Elemental, or Realm — these categories describe what conditions and effects they cause, not how much damage they deal.

A weapon can only have one damage type. If an ability adds damage, it doesn't change the type unless specified. When you roll repeated non-stacking conditions, deal +1 damage instead.

Cleave — Deal half damage to other creatures within melee range. Siphon — Heal yourself for the amount listed.

Physical Damage

✊ Impact

The raw force of blunt weapons such as hammers, maces, and clubs delivers bone-crushing blows. Shatters defenses and can leave foes dazed and reeling.

  1. Broken
  2. Concussion
  3. Coughing
  4. Dislocation
  5. Prone
  6. Knockback (10ft)

🎯 Piercing

Sharp and precise, piercing damage comes from arrows, spears, and daggers — designed to penetrate armor and flesh alike, targeting vital points.

  1. Additional +1
  2. Additional +2
  3. Additional +3
  4. Additional +4
  5. Additional +5
  6. Pinned

⚔️ Slashing

Swords, axes, and claws cut through flesh and armor with sweeping motions, leaving open wounds and bleeding gashes.

  1. Additional +2
  2. Additional +3
  3. Bleeding
  4. Cleave
  5. Disarm
  6. Feint (Take Stance)

Elemental Damage

Corrosive substances eat away at armor and flesh. Represents alchemical concoctions, venomous creatures, and spells that dissolve whatever they touch.

🌑 Eclipse

Drawing power from shadows and dark magic, eclipse damage drains the essence of life and light. Encompasses necromancy, suffocating smoke, and the chill of pure darkness.

  2. Additional +2

  5. Raise upon Death

Flames and explosions unleash intense heat, burning everything in their path — the fiery fury of fireballs, molten lava, and blazing infernos.

  5. Concussion

Realm Damage

🌿 Nature

Harnesses the primal forces of the natural world — plants, animals, and the elements. Manifests as thorny vines, swarming insects, or relentless wind and rain.

  1. Additional +1
  2. Constrained
  3. Confusion
  4. Coughing
  5. Disease (Verdant Fever)
  6. Pinned

✨ Psychic

Delves into the mind, bypassing physical defenses to inflict mental torment. Represents telepathic attacks, illusions, and the twisting of thoughts.

  1. Additional +1
  2. Blind
  3. Charmed
  4. Concussion
  5. Confusion
  6. Fear

☠️ Vozian

Born from decay, hate, and chaos, Vozian damage embodies corrupting disorder and malevolence. Spreads disease and rot, unraveling order and leaving a lingering taint on the soul.

  1. Siphon (Half Damage)
  2. Blind
  3. Coughing
  4. Disease (Infernal Skritches)
  5. Intangible (1 round)
  6. Double Damage

❤️‍🩹 Healing

Unlike other damage types, healing repairs and restores, mending wounds and revitalizing the body. Draws upon positive energy and restorative magic. Cannot cure conditions or ailments without special intervention.
