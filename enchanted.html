Enchanted items

You may find in your adventures and mysteries items that have been enchanted or crafted with special power. Each one carries a story. What force was used to forge it? Who created it? A forgotten mage, a desperate soul, or something older? And for what purpose: protection, vengeance, or something darker?

Unlike standard gear, these relics may evolve, whisper secrets, or alter fate itself. Some resonate with specific users. Others demand sacrifice or influence your dreams. They might be remnants of fallen gods, cursed inventions, or soul-bound curiosities that grow as you do. Every enchanted item is a piece of the world, and the more you learn about it, the more it might learn about you.

Jawbone Knuckles

The jawbone of a serial killer. Grants the user spirit and brutal force... but it whispers. Constantly. (Uses the Haunt mechanic)

Bruiser Gauntlets

These gauntlets harness the user's strength for close combat, amplifying each punch with raw force.

Upgrade: Elemental Sockets – Add fire, ice, or lightning damage once per short rest.

Skeletal Dagger

Deals 2 Eclipse damage. On hit against a living creature, summons a PL0 spectral skeleton for 1 minute.

Upgrade: Spectral Chain – Skeletons may replicate when they kill, forming a brief ghostly horde.

Extra Arm Mutation

Grow an additional arm, able to hold a small weapon and grant an extra off-action attack.

Upgrade: Enhanced Mutation – The arm can now wield a one-handed weapon and has an additional 1d4 strength, enhancing both damage and versatility.

Bag of Beasts

This enchanted bag spawns a creature from a d6 category table, allowing some choice. The creature's Power Level (PL) is determined by rolling 1d4-1.

Upgrade: Expanded Bestiary – The bag now has a d8 table of creatures and allows the player to roll 1d6-1 for PL, increasing the variety and potential strength of summoned creatures.

Tracker Glasses

These glasses allow the wearer to mark a target within medium range, tracking them across the same plane for up to 7 days. Only one target can be tracked at a time.

Upgrade: Long-Range Vision – The glasses’ range is extended, and the user can now track two targets at once, marking each with a different colored glow only visible to the wearer.

Multiclass Gear

Equipment that adapts and enhances abilities across multiple classes, designed to grow with the character.

Upgrade: Adaptive Module – The gear can now gain one additional feature from a second class each time it is upgraded, allowing full integration of a secondary class into the wearer’s skill set.

Cursed Gear

Items with powerful effects but significant drawbacks or conditions for use.

Upgrade: Controlled Curse – The user learns to control the curse, gaining advantage on rolls to avoid its negative effects. They can also attempt to temporarily remove the curse’s drawback once per day.

Template for Progression Magic Items

A framework for magical items that grow and evolve with the user.

Upgrade: Soulbound Template – Items using this template gain personalized traits and abilities based on the user’s experiences, ensuring that each item remains unique and continuously relevant.

Mutant Serum

A vial of liquid that grants the user a random mutant ability for one hour, chosen from a d6 table (healing, strength, resistance, etc.).

Upgrade: Permanent Mutation – With repeated use, the serum begins to have permanent effects, allowing the user to roll twice on the mutation table and choose one each time they drink it.

Demon Blood

When infused into an alchemical drink, demon blood grants extra damage resistance but requires a survival check to avoid terrifying demon screeches.

Upgrade: Demonic Fortitude – The user gains the ability to withstand the screeches without checks, and the blood provides temporary hit points along with its resistance.

Eclipse Cloak

This cloak grants +2 stealth bonus and allows the wearer to turn invisible for 1 minute. In The Eclipse, it enables the user to teleport as an action through shadows within short range.

Phoenix Feather Amulet

Grants immunity to fire damage and resurrects the wearer to 1 hit point upon death, destroying the amulet in the process.

Upgrade: Eternal Flame – The amulet’s resurrection effect can now be used twice before destruction, and it grants a small fire aura that deals minor damage to enemies within 5 feet.

Elemental Gauntlet

These gloves allow the user to release an elemental blast three times per day, with damage type chosen by the wearer.

Upgrade: Focused Burst – The gauntlet’s elemental blast now deals extra 1d4 damage, and it recharges on a short rest instead of once per day.

Mirror Shard Pendant

The wearer can create a temporary mirror clone once per day with half their health, useful for distractions or basic attacks.

Upgrade: Reflective Shield – The pendant can now create two clones, each lasting for up to 5 minutes, and they can defend the user, redirecting attacks with a slight reduction in damage.

Void Lantern

Captures small souls or spirits, releasing them to summon a spectral ally or access their memories.

Upgrade: Ethereal Warden – The lantern now holds up to two spirits at once, granting stronger allies or insights, and it glows with a protective aura that provides slight resistance to dark magic.

Ethereal Bandolier

Holds three vials of gas that create illusions when thrown, lasting 1d4 minutes.

Upgrade: Dream Mirage – The bandolier can now hold five vials, and the illusions gain limited physical form, able to distract or lightly restrain enemies for a brief period.

Harbinger’s Horn

Summons a spectral horse, with a stronger steed when used in The Eclipse.

Upgrade: Herald of the Eclipse – The horn now allows the steed to travel at incredible speed, and the rider becomes intangible for a brief time while mounted.

Jewel of the Deep

Grants underwater breathing and allows communication with aquatic creatures, as well as summoning a water elemental.

Upgrade: Tidal Surge – The wearer can now summon two water elementals once per long rest, and they gain the ability to swim at double speed.

Moonstone Circlet

Grants +1 to wisdom and allows visions of the past under moonlight.

Upgrade: Lunar Vision – The circlet now provides +2 to wisdom, and the user can receive prophetic dreams, glimpsing possible future events in addition to past ones.

Chimeric Bracers

These bracers allow the wearer to adopt an animalistic trait, such as claws, enhanced hearing, or night vision, for one hour, once per day.

Upgrade: Beastmaster Bond – The wearer can now activate two traits at once, and the bracers’ effect lasts up to two hours. Additionally, they may take on the form of a single powerful creature for 10 minutes, once per long rest.

Soulkeeper Blade

A blade that absorbs the souls of those it kills, granting +1 Strength per soul absorbed (up to 5 souls). The wielder can release the souls in a powerful attack, dealing 3d10 damage but causing fatigue.

Upgrade: Soul Harvest – The blade now holds up to 10 souls, granting +2 Strength per soul (max +10). Releasing the souls now has a chance to heal the wielder by 1d6 per soul released.

Essence Infuser

A mystical gem embedded into a bracer or necklace that amplifies the user’s elemental energy. The player selects a damage type (fire, ice, lightning, etc.) aligned with their personal story, which lasts for a brief time.

Upgrade: Resonant Crystal – Activate twice per encounter and switch elements between uses. Damage bonus increased and grants slight resistance to the chosen element while active.

Shapeshifter’s Cloak

This cloak grants temporary resistance to an environmental damage type, such as fire, ice, or lightning, depending on the wearer’s surroundings.

Upgrade: Elemental Ward – Grants full immunity to the chosen element and allows one element switch per day to adapt to changing conditions.

Weapon of Memories

A weapon that lets the wielder channel a memory tied to an element, imbuing attacks with that elemental energy for the duration of a battle.

Upgrade: Echo of the Past – Each memory grants unique elemental effects. Choose between two memories on activation. Store more memories as experiences are collected.

Stormcaller Gauntlets

These gauntlets allow the wearer to channel elemental energy based on their emotions, such as fire for anger or ice for calm.

Upgrade: Storm’s Fury – Channel two elements at once. Creates a lingering aura that deals minor ongoing elemental damage to nearby enemies.

Elemental Prism

A crystalline prism that absorbs energy from the user’s surroundings, allowing them to select an elemental type for added effects in spellcasting or attacks.

Upgrade: Prismatic Core – Can hold two elements simultaneously, combining them for hybrid damage and special effects against creatures vulnerable to either.

Bonded Totem

This totem stores elemental energy based on places the user has visited or creatures encountered, infusing their attacks with the chosen element.

Upgrade: Totem of the Ancients – Stores up to three elements and cycles between them during an encounter. Switching emits a pulse that briefly stuns nearby enemies.

Warrior’s Ember

A flame within a piece of jewelry that responds to the wearer’s intent, imbuing their attacks with an element of their choice.

Upgrade: Inferno Heart – Split the flame to apply two elemental effects with increased damage. Gain temporary resistance to one chosen element during use.

Spellbinder’s Staff

A staff that attunes to different elements based on the caster’s alignment, enhancing spells and adapting to various styles of magic.

Upgrade: Arcanist’s Resonance – Stores up to three elemental types and switches freely. Spells gain bonus effects like increased range or minor area-of-effect.

Whispering Blade

A weapon that resonates with the user’s spirit, channeling an elemental energy of their choice.

Upgrade: Voice of the Elements – Grants temporary mastery over the element, summoning creatures or creating hazards. Effects last the encounter and shape the battlefield.

Traveler’s Lantern

A lantern that collects elemental energy from the user’s travels, casting a glow that empowers allies or damages enemies.

Upgrade: Wayfarer’s Light – Holds two types of elemental energy. Its glow now also grants minor healing or movement bonuses to allies.
