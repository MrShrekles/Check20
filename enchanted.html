About Enchanted Items

You may find in your adventures and mysteries items that have been enchanted or crafted with special power. Each one carries a story - what force was used to forge it? Who created it? A forgotten mage, a desperate soul, or something older?

Unlike standard gear, these relics may evolve, whisper secrets, or alter fate itself. Some resonate with specific users. Others demand sacrifice or influence your dreams.

About Progression Items

Progression items are bound to a specific wielder and grow with them over 10 steps. Apply the enhancement to any eligible item, then unlock each step through play, milestones, or narrator approval. They are not found in shops - they are earned.
