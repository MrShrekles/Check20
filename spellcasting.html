📜 Spellcasting Rules Reference
Requirements

Spellcasting requires voice and gesture. Restraints or silence hinders magic.

Casting

Make a Spellcasting check (or Spirit). Default cost is 1 Action. Double SP for Half/Off-Action. Add +1 SP to no-cast spells.

Targeting

Requires sensory perception of the target.

Spell Points

Recover 1 on Press On. Recover all on Long Rest. Requires Spirit 2+ to cast (grants 1 SP).

Summoning

Cannot summon more creatures than half your Spellcasting check.

Conjure Location

Manifest spells must target unoccupied space. No conjuring mid-air unless the creature flies.
