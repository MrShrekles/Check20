Spellcasting

In the intricate dance of spellcasting, every movement and utterance matters. A mage must channel their intentions through voice and gesture, like a maestro guiding an orchestra. Yet, magic demands clarity. If a caster finds themselves silenced or restrained, the symphony of magic halts. Even their gear plays a role; while hands must be free as the wind, a shield may hinder, and armor can stifle the very currents of magic they seek to harness.

  • Requirements: Spellcasting requires voice and gesture. Restraints or silence hinders magic.
  • Casting: Make a Spellcasting check. If you lack a specific Spellcasting check, use a Spirit check. Spells generally require an action to cast or double the spell points for half-actions or off-actions. Add +1 to no-cast spells.
  • Targeting: Requires sensory perception of the target.
  • Spell Points: Recover 1 when you press on and all on a long rest.
  • Prerequisites: Requires a minimum Spirit check of 2, granting 1 spell point.

Intent Spellcasting

Embrace a revolutionary approach to magic that transcends the mundane confines of traditional spellcraft. Why limit yourself to stale, prewritten incantations that achieve only a singular effect? With dynamic spellcasting, you become the architect of your own spells, focusing on the profound how and what of your magical endeavors.

Crafting Your Spell

Manner and Transmission: Begin by selecting the Manner, the method by which your will influences the world. Pair it with a Transmission, the elemental or conceptual force you wish to wield.

Declare Your Intent Level: Choose from six ascending tiers of power, ranging from the gentle Whisper to the awe-inspiring Cataclysm. These levels dictate the magnitude of your spell, scaling from 1 to 24 Spell Points.

Manner

Decay

Pain, Damage, Erode, Deteriorate, Weaken, Disintegrate, Corrode

Discern

Sense, Perceive, Detect, Insight, Awareness

Domineer

Control, Influence, Command, Dominate, Overpower

Manifest

Create, Craft, Summon, Shape, Build

Transmission

Aether

Light, Spirit, Music, Vibration, Noise, Radiation, Solar, Vision, Shadows

Astral

Time, Portal, Speed, Slow, Chronology, Distance, Space, Teleportation

Corpus

Life, Harm, Blood, Emotion, Thought, Perception, Influence, Vitality, Soul

Elemental

Fire, Heat, Cold, Frost, Water, Ice, Steam, Acid, Poison, Earth, Lava, Wind

Material

Metal, Wood, Stone, Clay, Earth, Plants, Tools, Weapons, Structures, Minerals

Spell Intent

After declaring the Manner and Transmission of your spell, you must select its Intent Level, which determines the spell's potency and the depth of its impact. Each Intent Level requires a specific amount of Spell Points and allows you to deal damage or inflict conditions categorized as Corpus, Cognition, or Major Conditions.

  • all spells require an action to cast
  • all spells require a check, either a Spirit check or a relavent spellcasting check determined by your class

Whisper

Cost: 1 Spell Point

Small objects, tiny elemental effects, light damage, basic illusions, or lights.

  • Spell Attack: Deal 1d6 + 1 damage or inflict a Corpus condition.
  • Spell Detonation: Deal half of 1d6 + 1 damage to all creatures within short range

Surge

Cost: 3 Spell Points

Move medium objects, open doors, create small elemental effects, or enhance abilities.

  • Spell Attack: Deal 2d6 + 3 damage or inflict a Corpus condition and then deal the damage on a success+.
  • Spell Detonation: Deal half of 2d6 + 3 damage to all creatures within short range

Shout

Cost: 6 Spell Points

Move large objects, break barriers, create medium elemental effects, moderate healing, or protection.

  • Spell Attack: Deal 4d6 + 6 damage or inflict a Corpus or Cognition condition and then deal the damage on a success+.
  • Spell Detonation: Deal half of 4d6 + 6 damage to all creatures within short range

Roar

Cost: 9 Spell Points

Devastate battlefields, summon large creatures, moderate area healing, or topple walls.

  • Spell Attack: Deal 9d6 + 9 damage or inflict a Corpus or Cognition condition and then deal the damage on a success+.
  • Spell Detonation: Deal half of 9d6 + 9 damage to all creatures within short range

Storm

Cost: 12 Spell Points

Collapse buildings, summon powerful creatures, large-scale destruction, or long-lasting barriers.

  • Spell Attack: Deal 12d6 + 12 damage and inflict a Corpus condition or inflict a Major condition then deal the damage on a success+.
  • Spell Detonation: Deal half of 12d6 + 12 damage to all creatures within medium range.

Cataclysm

Cost: 24 Spell Points

Affect expansive areas, summon immense storms, reshape terrain, raise the dead, or potentially grant wishes.

  • Spell Attack: Deal 24d6 + 24 damage and inflict a Corpus or Cognition condition or inflict a Major condition and then deal the damage on a success+.
  • Spell Detonation: Deal half of 24d6 + 24 damage to all creatures within long range.
