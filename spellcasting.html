Spellcasting

In the intricate dance of spellcasting, every movement and utterance matters. A mage must channel their intentions through voice and gesture, like a maestro guiding an orchestra. Yet, magic demands clarity. If a caster finds themselves silenced or restrained, the symphony of magic halts. Even their gear plays a role; while hands must be free as the wind, a shield may hinder, and armor can stifle the very currents of magic they seek to harness.

  • Requirements: Spellcasting requires voice and gesture. Restraints or silence hinders magic.
  • Casting: Make a Spellcasting check. If you lack a specific Spellcasting check, use a Spirit check. Spells generally require an action to cast or double the spell points for half-actions or off-actions. Add +1 to no-cast spells.
  • Targeting: Requires sensory perception of the target.
  • Spell Points: Recover 1 when you press on and all on a long rest.
  • Prerequisites: Requires a minimum Spirit check of 2, granting 1 spell point.

Intent Spellcasting

Embrace a revolutionary approach to magic that transcends the mundane confines of traditional spellcraft. Why limit yourself to stale, prewritten incantations that achieve only a singular effect? With dynamic spellcasting, you become the architect of your own spells, focusing on the profound how and what of your magical endeavors.

Crafting Your Spell

Manner and Transmission: Begin by selecting the Manner, the method by which your will influences the world. Pair it with a Transmission, the elemental or conceptual force you wish to wield.

Declare Your Intent Level: Choose from six ascending tiers of power, ranging from the gentle Whisper to the awe-inspiring Cataclysm. These levels dictate the magnitude of your spell, scaling from 1 to 24 Spell Points.

Spell Rules

  • Summoning: You cannot summon an amount of creatures higher than half your spellcsting check
  • Where to Conjure: You can only Manifest or target spells that bring in struuctures in an unnoccupied space. You cannot conjure in flying space unless its a flying creature.

Manner

  • Decay: Pain, Damage, Erode, Deteriorate, Weaken, Disintegrate, Corrode
  • Discern: Sense, Perceive, Detect, Insight, Awareness
  • Domineer: Control, Influence, Command, Dominate, Overpower
  • Manifest: Create, Craft, Summon, Shape, Build

Transmission

  • Aether: Light, Spirit, Music, Vibration, Noise, Radiation, Solar, Vision, Shadows
  • Astral: Time, Portal, Speed, Slow, Chronology, Distance, Space, Teleportation
  • Corpus: Life, Harm, Blood, Emotion, Thought, Perception, Influence, Vitality, Soul
  • Elemental: Fire, Heat, Cold, Frost, Water, Ice, Steam, Acid, Poison, Earth, Lava, Wind
  • Material: Metal, Wood, Stone, Clay, Tools, Weapons, Structures, Minerals, Constructs, Gears
  • Verdance: Growth, Crops, Plants, Roots, Vines, Fungi, Terrain, Seasons, Animals, Mold

Spell Intent

Each spell uses Manner, Transmission, and Intent. After choosing Manner and Transmission, select an Intent Level to determine the spell's power, scale, and cost.

  • All spells require an action to cast.
  • All spells require a check (Spirit or your class's spellcasting check).
  • Spells may deal damage or inflict Corpus, Cognition, or Major Conditions.
Spell Point Costs
  • Whisper: 1 SP
  • Surge: 3 SP
  • Shout: 6 SP
  • Roar: 9 SP
  • Storm: 12 SP
  • Cataclysm: 24 SP
Damage Template
  • Spell Attack: xd6 + x damage or apply a condition
  • Spell Detonation: Half of xd6 + x damage to all in range
  • X = Intent Level
Effect Scale by Intent
  • Whisper: Small objects, tiny elemental effects, light damage, basic illusions, or lights.
  • Surge: Move medium objects, open doors, create small elemental effects, or enhance abilities
  • Shout: Move large objects, break barriers, create medium elemental effects, moderate healing, or protection.
  • Roar: Devastate battlefields, summon large creatures, moderate area healing, or topple walls.
  • Storm: Collapse buildings, summon powerful creatures, large-scale destruction, or long-lasting barriers.
  • Cataclysm: Affect expansive areas, summon immense storms, reshape terrain, raise the dead, or potentially grant wishes.
