Weapon & Armor Crafting

In the world of Shek, crafting armor and weapons requires a Forge Workbench, materials (purchased or scavenged), and skilled hands. Existing mundane items can also be repurposed into new items, such as converting heavy armor into medium armor or reshaping medium armor into a weapon.

Glyphs

Glyphs

Glyphs are powerful symbols infused with magic, capable of granting weapons, armor, and even characters with remarkable abilities and enhancements. Through glyphs, a simple piece of gear can become a legendary artifact, and a character may access abilities that significantly alter their role and capabilities within the game.

Crafting and Application

  • Implementation: Crafting and applying glyphs require specific materials and a suitable crafting station, such as an enchanting table or magical lab. The rarity of materials and quality of the station can affect the outcome.
  • Crafting Process: Crafters must perform Crafting, Intellect, Spirit, or other related checks. The complexity of the glyph determines the number of successes required.
  • Application Process: Applying a glyph involves a similar check to integrate the glyph's magical essence fully. Each step takes one hour, with more complex glyphs requiring additional time.
  • Risk of Failure: Failed crafting or application checks can damage or destroy the item, with consequences scaling based on the severity of the failure.

Glyph Tiers

‚ú® Basic Glyphs

Enhance damage, armor, or checks, ideal for incremental improvements to gear.

Requirements: 1 check to craft, 1 to apply; each step takes an hour and can be done with minimal equipment.

  • Glyph of Fortification: Increases armor by +2.
  • Glyph of Sharpness: Adds +1 to damage rolls on bladed weapons.

  • Glyph of Swiftness: Increases movement speed by +20ft when applied to footwear.
  • Glyph of Silence: Silences weapons or armor, ideal for stealthy characters.

üåü Legendary Glyphs

  • Glyph of Transformation: Allows the character to shift into a specific creature, gaining its abilities temporarily.
  • Glyph of Time Manipulation: once per long rest, take 2 actions on your turn.
  • Glyph of Elemental Mastery: Grants control over a specific element for strategic advantages.
