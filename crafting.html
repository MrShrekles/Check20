▼ Weapon & Armor Crafting Forge Workbench

Crafting armor and weapons requires a Forge Workbench, materials (purchased or scavenged), and skilled hands. Existing mundane items can also be repurposed - converting heavy armor into medium armor or reshaping medium armor into a weapon.

Forge Workbench

A Forge Workbench includes a main station where the crafter makes their Crafting check. Up to three allies can assist using different workstations.

🔥 Forge Strength or Survival check
⚒️ Anvil Strength or Agility check
🧵 Tailoring Station Intellect or Influence check

Crafting Process

  • Crafting Time: A long downtime or long rest in a safe place with a Forge Workbench.
  • Making Checks: The main crafter and allies each make their respective checks. The total successes determine the item's quality.

Glyphs
▼ Glyphs - Overview Enchantment

Glyphs are powerful symbols infused with magic, capable of granting weapons, armor, and even characters with remarkable abilities. Through glyphs, a simple piece of gear can become a legendary artifact.

Crafting & Application

  • Implementation: Crafting and applying glyphs require specific materials and a suitable station such as an enchanting table or magical lab.
  • Crafting Process: Crafters must perform Crafting, Intellect, Spirit, or other related checks. The complexity of the glyph determines the number of successes required.
  • Application Process: Applying a glyph involves a similar check to integrate the glyph's magical essence fully. Each step takes one hour.
  • Risk of Failure: Failed checks can damage or destroy the item, with consequences scaling based on the severity of the failure.

▼ ✨ Basic Glyphs 1 check · 1 hour

Enhance damage, armor, or checks - ideal for incremental improvements to gear. Requires 1 check to craft and 1 to apply; can be done with minimal equipment.

Glyph of FortificationIncreases armor by +2.
Glyph of SharpnessAdds +1 to damage rolls on bladed weapons.
▼ ⚔️ Advanced Glyphs 2 checks · Workstation

Introduce new mechanics like special attacks, defensive maneuvers, or unique effects. Requires 2 checks to craft and apply; requires a proper workstation and additional materials or assistance.

Glyph of ReboundingReflects half of damage back to the attacker as an off-action (reaction).
Glyph of SwiftnessIncreases movement speed by +20ft when applied to footwear.
Glyph of SilenceSilences weapons or armor, ideal for stealthy characters.
▼ 🌟 Legendary Glyphs 3 checks · Team effort

Provide progression systems and grant game-changing abilities or major character transformations. Requires 3 checks to craft and apply; requires a powerful workstation and a team effort.

Glyph of TransformationAllows the character to shift into a specific creature, gaining its abilities temporarily.
Glyph of Time ManipulationOnce per long rest, take 2 actions on your turn.
Glyph of Elemental MasteryGrants control over a specific element for strategic advantages.
